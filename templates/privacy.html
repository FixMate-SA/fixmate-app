Privacy Policy for FixMate-SA

Last Updated: July 1, 2025

Donald Shai Technologies (Pty) Ltd ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our FixMate-SA platform ("Platform"). This policy is drafted in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information necessary to provide our service, including:

  • From Clients: Contact details (WhatsApp number, name), job details (service descriptions, location), and feedback data (ratings, comments).
  • From Fixers: Identity and contact details, skills, vetting information, and real-time location data during an active job.

2. How We Use Your Information (Purpose of Processing)

Your information is used to connect Clients with Fixers, process payments, maintain safety and quality, improve our platform, and communicate with you about your service requests.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We only share information when necessary to provide the service. A Client's job details and contact info are shared with the assigned Fixer. A Fixer's name is shared with the Client. We do not sell your personal information.

4. Your Rights Under POPIA

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information, and to object to its processing. To do so, please contact our Information Officer.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact our designated Information Officer:

  • Name: Donald Shai
